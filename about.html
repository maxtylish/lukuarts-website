關於我

用光影捕捉故事的情感溫度

LUKUARTS VISUAL is a photography brand focusing on portrait and commercial photography. To me, photography is not just about pressing the shutter; it's a way of observing the world.

Through the delicate capture of light, shadow, composition, and emotion, I am dedicated to transforming every moment into a timeless visual story. Whether it's a warm personal portrait, a highly textured commercial project, or a high-speed track day event, I deliver the highest quality visual solutions.

青玥影像 (LUKUARTS VISUAL) 是一個專注於人像與商業攝影的影像品牌。對我而言，攝影不只是按下快門，而是一種觀察世界的方式。

透過光影、構圖與情感的細膩捕捉，我致力於將每一個瞬間轉化為雋永的視覺故事。無論是充滿溫度的個人肖像、強調質感的商業企劃，還是熱血的高速賽事紀錄，我都能提供最高品質的影像方案。
