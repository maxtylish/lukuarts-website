About Me

用光影捕捉故事的情感溫度

青玥影像（LUKUARTS VISUAL）是一個專注於人像與商業攝影的影像品牌。對我而言，攝影不只是按下快門，而是一種觀察世界的方式。透過對光影的細膩捕捉與構圖的嚴謹要求，我致力於讓每一張照片都能傳遞出最純粹的氛圍與故事。

無論是唯美的人像、充滿溫度的婚禮紀錄，還是充滿質感的商業攝影，我始終相信「真誠」是影像的核心。我希望在畫面中保留最自然的真實瞬間，讓影像不只是瞬間的定格，而是一段能被反覆回味的珍貴記憶。

LUKUARTS VISUAL is a boutique photography brand specializing in portrait and commercial storytelling. For me, photography is more than just a craft—it’s a perspective. I believe that through the delicate interplay of light and shadow, every image can evoke a deep sense of time and emotion.

Whether capturing intimate portraits, heartfelt wedding stories, or sophisticated commercial aesthetics, my philosophy remains the same: authenticity is paramount. My mission is to preserve the natural essence of every moment, turning fleeting glimpses into timeless visual legacies.

專業領域： Portrait / Commercial / Events
