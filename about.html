About Me

Vision Beyond the Shutter

Hello, I'm Liu Chih-Heng. At LUKUARTS VISUAL, photography is more than just pressing the shutter—it's the refinement of visual storytelling. From intimate emotional portraits to high-impact commercial visuals, I focus on striking the perfect balance between light, shadow, color, and composition.

My true edge lies in the absolute control of atmospheric mood. To me, an image is never just a simple record; it is an extension of creation and artistic vision. I am dedicated to sculpting every single frame with a distinct cinematic quality. I also thrive on capturing high-dynamic moments, with expertise spanning from quiet humanistic documentaries to high-speed sports photography.

Beyond still images, I provide professional videography services. Utilizing the Nikon Z system paired with top-tier audio equipment, along with precise color grading and seamless audio processing, I ensure that every project delivers an immersive, dual-sensory impact.
